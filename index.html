About Us

Formula One (F1) is the highest class of international single-seater auto racing. It is organized by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA) and features the fastest regulated racing cars in the world.

In simple terms: Formula 1 is the top global racing competition where the fastest drivers and teams compete to become world champions.
